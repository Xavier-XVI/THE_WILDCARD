## REVIEW PHASE

**[AGENT]**

Here's what I understood about how you run your practice:

---

**What I Heard**

You run a solo life coaching practice serving individuals across four tracks — career transition, personal development, executive growth, and relocation. The Positive Intelligence Program runs separately as a 6-8 week cohort, twice a year. At any given time you carry 8-12 one-on-one clients in 3-month packages of 6 sessions, paid upfront.

Your intro session conversion rate is high — 70-75% — because the people who show up are mostly pre-qualified by trust.

Once someone becomes a client, you run structured-but-flexible sessions: start by identifying what the client wants from this conversation, spend the bulk in exploratory dialogue, close with concrete focus goals. Notes go into a per-client Google Doc you review before every session. Clients don't receive a written record of their focus goals unless you choose to send one — and that's inconsistent. Between-session check-ins are high-value but ad hoc.

Engagements close naturally — some clients self-renew, some return months later. No formal offboarding, no systematic testimonial collection, no structured re-engagement path.

The thing you'd most want running on its own tomorrow: the post-intro follow-up email. The thing that runs on infrastructure and memory that looks more polished than it is: all of it.
